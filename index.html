✦ The Theory

Why Your Bias Tells the Truth

Attraction isn't random. Psychology tells us we're drawn to people who reflect something real about our inner world.

🪞
The Mirror Effect
We recognize our own traits in others before we consciously acknowledge them in ourselves. Your bias may be showing you who you already are.
✨
Idealization
We project our aspirational self onto people we admire. The qualities you idolize most are often the ones you most want to develop.
🌑
The Shadow Side
Every archetype has a shadow — the flip side of its strength. Understanding yours is the beginning of real self-awareness.
🧲
Magnetic Attraction
What draws you in isn't always what you expect. Sometimes we're pulled toward someone who represents what we've suppressed in ourselves.
