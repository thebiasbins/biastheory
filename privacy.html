1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Bias Theory ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit biastheory.net, take the quiz, submit a form, purchase a Deep Dive Read, or subscribe to our mailing list.

By using the site you agree to the practices described here. If you have questions, email us at hello@biastheory.net.

2. What we collect and why

Here is a plain-language breakdown of what information we collect, how we get it, and what we do with it:

What How Why
Quiz answers and result data Entered by you when taking the quiz To generate your personality read. Not stored on our servers — processed in your browser and passed to the AI generation service.
Email address Voluntarily provided in suggestion forms or email signup To respond to your message or send the newsletter you signed up for. Never required to use the site.
Suggestion and feedback submissions Submitted via the form on the site To read and act on your feedback. Stored in Formspree. Includes any information you choose to include.
Payment information Entered via the Stripe payment form for Deep Dives To process your purchase. We never see or store your card details — Stripe handles all payment data directly.
Basic analytics Collected automatically via Google Analytics if enabled To understand how the site is being used (which pages are visited, where traffic comes from). Anonymized.

What we do not collect: We do not collect your name unless you provide it. We do not store quiz results on our servers. We do not track your behavior across other websites. We do not build profiles tied to your identity.

3. How your quiz data is handled

When you take the Bias Theory quiz, your answers are processed entirely in your browser. Your results and dimension scores are only sent to Anthropic's API (to generate your Deep Dive read if you purchase one) and are not stored on Bias Theory's servers.

When you purchase a Deep Dive Read, your quiz data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your personalized report. This transmission is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy. The generated read is returned to your browser and not retained by us.

Bias Theory does not store, sell, or analyze individual quiz results for any purpose other than generating the product you requested.

4. Cookies and analytics

Bias Theory may use Google Analytics to understand site traffic in aggregate — things like how many people visit, which pages are most popular, and where visitors come from. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not participate in behavioral advertising networks.

If you prefer not to be tracked by Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-party services

Bias Theory uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy and data practices:

  • Stripe — processes payments for Deep Dive Reads. Stripe stores your payment details on their secure servers. We never see your full card number. stripe.com/privacy
  • Anthropic — generates AI content for Deep Dive Reads. Your quiz data is sent to their API for content generation. anthropic.com/privacy
  • Formspree — receives and stores your suggestion and feedback form submissions. formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Mailchimp — manages email subscriptions if you sign up for our mailing list. mailchimp.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Analytics — provides anonymized site traffic data. policies.google.com/privacy

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your email address from our mailing list at any time — every email we send includes an unsubscribe link
  • Request deletion of any form submission or personal data you've shared with us by emailing hello@biastheory.net
  • Opt out of analytics tracking using your browser settings or the Google opt-out tool

We will respond to data requests within 30 days. We do not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

7. Children's privacy

Bias Theory is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@biastheory.net and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted to us. The site operates over HTTPS, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe (PCI-compliant), and we do not store sensitive personal or financial data on our own servers.

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site grows or as legal requirements change. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the site after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
